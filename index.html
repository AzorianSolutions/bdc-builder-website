About the Tool

While the tool itself isn't fancy, it is however simple and reliable as a result. Running the tool is no sweat, gathering the input data is most of the work. The tool requires only four CSV files for input, and an arbitrary number of coverage models to meet your desired level of detail.

The CostQuest location fabric is the first of the four input data sets required. Next, is a simple CSV file containing metadata about each coverage model you will use. Your service plans are the third data set required, and lastly, your subscription data. In many cases, most of this can be exported from your operational support system (OSS).
